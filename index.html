LET'S ACT

English theatre for kids

WHAT IS THIS?

Texto

In this project, students will create and perform a small play in English for younger children in Kindergarten. The theme is about theatre, stories, animals, emotions, and daily routines.

The students will work in groups and each person will have a role, like writer, actor, or video helper. First, they will write a simple script in English. Then, they will practise speaking, pronunciation, and acting. Finally, they will perform the play and record it on video to share with the school.

During the project, students will improve their English speaking and writing skills, learn to work as a team, and use technology in a fun way.

The project will last about 3 weeks, with 8 to 10 lessons. It connects English, Art, and Spanish Language classes.

The main goal is to learn English in a creative and real situation while having fun and helping younger students enjoy theatre.

Some applications to use in class: Symbaloo
